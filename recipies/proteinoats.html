Dawg Bowl

Description

This will pack the protein into your frail body. The God's themselves couldn't think of a more nutritious dish.

Ingredients

  • 2 scoops of chocolate protein powder [60g protein]
  • 160g quaker oats
  • 16oz 1% Milk [20g protein]

Instructions

  1. Place 2 scoops of chocolate protein powder into a large bowl
  2. Place 160g of oats into bowl
  3. Add 16oz of milk and stir
  4. Enjoy
