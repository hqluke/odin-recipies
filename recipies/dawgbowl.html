Dawg Bowl

Description

This will pack the protein into your frail body. The God's themselves couldn't think of a more nutritious dish.

Ingredients

  • 1lb 90/10 grass fed ground beef [92g protein]
  • 4 servings of egg whites (46g per serving) [20g protein]
  • 200g steamed white rice (carbs for fuel)
  • 100g vanilla flavored greek yogurt(use one with 0% fat) [17g protein]

Instructions

  1. Place ground beef in a 12 inch pan and press down to spread it evenly
  2. Season your beef generously. You can't go wrong with salt, pepper, and garlic powder. I personally use a box of spices that's called "Carne Asada Seasoning"
  3. I honestly don't know how long I wait until I flip it I just feel it out. Maybe 5ish minutes? Once flipped, season the other side generously.
  4. Wait for a 2-3 minutes and add in your egg whites constantly stir the eggwhites with the beef so they mix well.
  5. Once your egg whites are cooked pretty well add in your rice and greek yogurt
  6. You can check to make sure the beef reaches 145 with a thermometer and then you're done
  7. Enjoy
